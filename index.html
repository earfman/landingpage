How ZeroTrustKerberosLink Works

Deploy in under 60 minutes with our step-by-step guided setup process

ZeroTrustKerberosLink provides a seamless authentication experience while enforcing Zero Trust security policies.

1

Enterprise Authentication

Users authenticate using their existing Kerberos credentials through your enterprise identity provider (Active Directory).

2

Zero Trust Verification

Our policy engine verifies user identity, device compliance, and contextual factors before granting access.

3

Secure AWS Access

Temporary AWS credentials are generated with appropriate permissions based on the user's identity and role.

4

Comprehensive Auditing

All authentication events and AWS resource access are logged and monitored for security analysis.

Our Story

Securing Enterprise Authentication Since 2020

Founded by security experts with decades of experience in enterprise authentication and cloud security

ZeroTrustKerberosLink was born out of a simple observation: enterprises were struggling to securely connect their existing Kerberos authentication infrastructure with modern AWS services.

Our founding team of security architects and cloud engineers had firsthand experience with this challenge while working at Fortune 500 companies. After seeing many organizations build costly, insecure custom solutions or compromise on data sovereignty with third-party SaaS offerings, we decided there had to be a better way.

In 2020, we set out to create a solution that would:

  • Maintain complete data sovereignty through self-hosting
  • Implement true Zero Trust principles
  • Deploy in minutes, not months
  • Meet enterprise compliance requirements

Today, ZeroTrustKerberosLink is trusted by security-conscious organizations across financial services, healthcare, government, and other regulated industries to securely bridge their Kerberos infrastructure with AWS services.

Our mission is to help enterprises maintain security and compliance while accelerating their cloud journey, without compromising on data sovereignty or control.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

1. Introduction

ZeroTrustKerberosLink ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ZeroTrustKerberosLink software and services.

2. Information We Collect

As a self-hosted solution, ZeroTrustKerberosLink is designed to minimize data collection. We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, and company information.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our product.
  • Technical Data: We collect information about your system environment for troubleshooting purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve ZeroTrustKerberosLink
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Understand how you use our service
  • Develop new products and services

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. ZeroTrustKerberosLink is designed with security at its core, following Zero Trust principles to ensure your data remains secure.

5. Self-Hosted Nature of Our Solution

ZeroTrustKerberosLink is primarily a self-hosted solution, which means:

  • Your authentication data remains within your infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your Kerberos tickets or AWS credentials
  • You maintain complete data sovereignty

6. Compliance

ZeroTrustKerberosLink is designed to help you maintain compliance with various regulatory requirements, including:

  • SOC2
  • HIPAA
  • GDPR
  • Other industry-specific regulations

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@zerotrustkerberoslink.com.

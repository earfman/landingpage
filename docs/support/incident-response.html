Incident Response Process

Our structured approach to handling security and service incidents

Incident Response Framework

ZeroTrustKerberosLink follows a comprehensive incident response framework designed to quickly identify, contain, and resolve security and service incidents. Our process is aligned with industry best practices and regulatory requirements.

Incident Response Phases

  1. Detection and Analysis - Rapid identification of potential incidents
  2. Containment - Immediate actions to limit impact
  3. Eradication - Removing the cause of the incident
  4. Recovery - Restoring systems to normal operation
  5. Post-Incident Analysis - Learning and improving

Customer Communication

We are committed to transparent communication during incidents:

  • Initial notification within 15 minutes of confirmed P1 incidents
  • Regular status updates throughout the incident lifecycle
  • Detailed post-incident reports within 48 hours

Contact During Incidents

During active incidents, customers can reach our incident response team through:

  • Emergency Hotline: +1 (800) 555-0911
  • Email: incident@zerotrustkerberoslink.com
  • Status Page: https://status.zerotrustkerberoslink.com
